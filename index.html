How I Can Help

Physical Therapy

Orthopedic specialist care for pain, injury, posture, movement dysfunction, and coordination. Evidence-based assessment and hands-on treatment to optimize how your body moves.

Breath Optimization

Breathing patterns deeply affect your physiology. I work with airway health, breathing mechanics, and breath capacity to improve both performance and everyday resilience.

Nervous System Regulation

Your nervous system controls movement, stress response, and healing. Through trauma-informed approaches, I help you access better regulation and nervous system resilience.

Services available in-person (Santa Cruz) and virtually, depending on your needs.

Who I Work With

If you're dealing with chronic pain, recovering from injury, concerned about posture or movement quality, struggling with anxiety or stress, or looking to optimize your breathing and nervous system — this is for you. I work with people who understand that real health is about the whole system, not just treating symptoms.

About Me

I'm an evidence-based physical therapist with a deep commitment to understanding movement, breathing, and nervous system function as interconnected systems. My training spans orthopedic rehabilitation, respiratory mechanics, and trauma-informed nervous system work.

I believe in meeting people where they are, blending rigorous clinical assessment with a holistic understanding of how your body works as a whole.

Based in Santa Cruz, I work both in-person and virtually, tailoring my approach to each person's unique needs and goals.
